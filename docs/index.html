Precision before spectacle

Elise builds programs around clean entrances, held edges, and musical restraint. Her strongest work happens in the space between jumps, where posture and pace make the rink feel larger.

Training is split between technique blocks, run-through stamina, and choreography sessions for competition and gala settings.
