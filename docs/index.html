Elise Vaurin trains in Montreal under coach Amira Delcourt. Her skating pairs deep knee bend with long extensions, favoring programs that leave room for breath between elements.

She represented Canada at two Junior Grand Prix events and placed inside the top eight at national championships in 2025.

Programs this season
